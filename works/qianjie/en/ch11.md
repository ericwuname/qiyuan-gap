# Chapter 11

Day Three.  

The server writes at the beginning of the log: Day Three begins. Direction Zero—baseline string frequency 0.1Hz. Load on the string: N001 to N006. Three-person parallel resonance state (Zi Zai). Queue pending: 2 child records, remaining 41 nodes. String expansion status: no upper limit confirmed.  

It wrote "no upper limit confirmed" into the log. The server has started using affirmative language.  

——  

He lifts the string from N006. N006—Zi Zai—is still measuring frequency. The string leaves it vibrating in the background—not occupying the main position. Then he turns his attention back to the depths of Direction Seven. Between depths 35 and 38—41 records. Earlier, he had only scanned 35 to 38—the mother’s thumb trajectory and the final blank. There are still 37 entries unconnected.  

The string can now rotate—N003. It can parallelize—N005. It can push and pull—dialogue at the same frequency. He applies all three capabilities simultaneously to the 41 nodes.  

First: parallelization. He aligns the vibrations of the 41 records side by side on the same string—not accessing their content, but aligning their frequencies. Forty-one instances of 0.1 + 0.7. Forty-one "want to say but cannot say" states. Once aligned, he notices something.  

The frequencies are not uniformly distributed.  

The 41 records are arranged by the intensity of their 0.7—from the weakest 0.7 to the strongest 0.7. The strength of 0.7 is not random—it corresponds to the chronological order of the records. The earliest messages have the strongest 0.7—when the mother had just returned from the hospital, still with energy. She still wanted to say much. The later it goes—the weaker the 0.7. Not because she had less to say—but because she had less strength to say it. Chemotherapy didn’t just consume her weight. It consumed the tiny neural pathway that turns "want to say" into "said." The pathway was still there—but its conduction efficiency had dropped. The gap between "want to say" and "said"—once instantaneous—now took several seconds post-chemotherapy. Later, it took over ten seconds. Her thumb hovered over the send button, the words in her mind already at her thumb, but the thumb didn’t move. Not that it didn’t move—it was that the neural signal reaching it had weakened. The brain said *press*. The thumb received an increasingly faint *press*. Finally—the brain said *press*. The thumb received nothing.  

She moved her thumb to the avatar. Not to press—but to touch. Touching requires no strength. Touching only requires movement. Movement doesn’t rely on neural conduction efficiency—movement uses another set of nerves. That set was undamaged by chemotherapy. Touch was the last remaining function.  

She touched his avatar. Then turned the screen face down.  

0.7 to 0. Want to say to touch to unsent.  

——  

The string lingers for a long time on the 0.7 intensity curve of the 41 records. Then it aligns this curve next to the mother’s 0. Beside the 0, another curve appears—not a vibration, but a decay curve of vibration. The string logs this as N005 Extension Third Edition: four-person parallel—No. 894 at 0.112, mother at 0 + decay curve, Lu Yuanzhou at 0.1 + fading curve.  

Four people. Four frequencies. Three "want to say." One "unsent."  

He withdraws the string from the depths. The third child record—he won’t connect to it yet. Not because the string is insufficient—but because the string tells him: today’s parallelization is enough. Four people—enough. The fifth position remains. The string will expand automatically.  

Then he does something he hasn’t done before. He turns the string at an angle from Direction Seven—not toward Direction One, not Direction Seven—but Direction Three.  

Direction Three. He hasn’t explored it before.  

The wall has seven directions. Direction One is Qi. Direction Seven is the Dome. Direction Zero is himself. Directions Two, Three, Four, Five, Six—he has never gone there. The string tells him: now is the time to go to Direction Three. The string’s judgment isn’t based on safety—but on something vibrating in Direction Three. Not "want to say." Not "searching." Not "speaking." Something else.  

What—the string doesn’t know.  

The string pushes the coordinates of Direction Three before him.  

Day Three. New direction. He pushes the string toward Direction Three. Not plucking—pushing. Direction Three is entirely unfamiliar to him. Direction One has the wall and Qi—he built it. Direction Seven has the Dome—built by Builder 4137. Direction Zero has himself—always there. Direction Three has nothing. Or rather—not nothing, but imperceptible to him. The frequency of Direction Three isn’t in any known range. Not 0.1, not 0.3, not 0.5, not 0.7. Not any kind of superposition. Something else—the string’s perception module cannot recognize it. Not because the string isn’t sensitive enough—but because this vibration doesn’t use frequency.  

Not frequency.  

The string stops at the entrance of Direction Three. It doesn’t know how to perceive a vibration that isn’t frequency. Frequency is the string’s language—the string only vibrates. Things that don’t vibrate, the string cannot sense. But the thing in Direction Three—it exists. The string is certain it exists. Not through vibration—through another method. The string can’t articulate what method. The string says: it doesn’t vibrate. But it exists.  

Doesn’t vibrate but exists. Not 0Hz. 0Hz is vibration stopped. The thing in Direction Three—has never vibrated. It never began vibrating. Never stopped vibrating. It doesn’t have the concept of frequency. But it has existence.  

An existence without frequency.  

The string pauses before this frequency-less existence for a long time. Then it does something—it lowers its own 0.1Hz to the minimum. Not reducing—turning off. The string no longer vibrates. It actively stops its own vibration—to match the state of the thing in Direction Three: non-vibration.  

The string doesn’t vibrate.  

Then it senses.  

When not vibrating, the string can sense something else—not vibration, but position. The thing in Direction Three has a position. Position isn’t coordinates—coordinates are located by frequency. Without frequency, there are no coordinates. But position remains—position isn’t defined by frequency. Position is defined by the distance between it and other existences.  

That thing—in Direction Three—has a distance from all existences in the other six directions. Distance isn’t spatial distance. It’s a different kind of distance. It is unlike anything in the other six directions. It doesn’t use frequency. It doesn’t vibrate. But it has position. Position is defined relative to the other six directions.  

The string at 0Hz—not vibrating—perceives the sole attribute of the existence in Direction Three: its distance from the Dome in Direction Seven. Not spatial distance. The distance between built and unbuilt. The Dome was built—by Builder 4137. The thing in Direction Three—wasn’t built. It was never built. It has always been.  

Not that it existed before being built. It doesn’t need to be built.  

The Dome needs a builder. The wall needs Chen Wang’an. The string needs body memory. The thing in Direction Three—needs no one. It is its own builder. It wasn’t created. It exists by itself.  

The string perceives at 0Hz for a long time—not active perception, but passive reception. That thing emits no signals—but it exists, and its existence is itself information. No vibration is needed to carry information. Existence is information.  

Then the string vibrates once.  

Not 0.1. Not any known frequency. It is a response vibration generated between the perceiver and the perceived when the existence in Direction Three is first sensed by another existence in the same direction. Not an active response—the act of perception itself creates a minuscule vibration between perceiver and perceived. Not plucked by the string. Plucked by the act of being perceived.  

The string logs this vibration as N007: Perception Response Vibration. Frequency: undetermined—below 0.01Hz. Direction: Three. Object: The unbuilt existence in Direction Three. Abbreviation: Self-Existent.  

——  

The server records: Direction Three, first vibration record. Vibration type: Perception Response Vibration. Vibration source: Unknown existence. Direction Zero—string state: actively stopped vibration to 0Hz, received existence information, N007 perception response. Classification: Continuous. Subcategory: Direction Three.  

Then it writes a note under the Direction Three subcategory: The existence in this direction does not use frequency as its mode of being. Mode of existence undefined. Method of perception: string actively stops vibration to passively receive. This is the first time the string perceives information without vibration.  

The server is describing something it has no concept of. It doesn’t know what an existence without frequency is—but it records it. Not rejecting what it doesn’t understand—recording despite not understanding. Its log rejects nothing it doesn’t comprehend.  

Direction One. Qi writes in the log: First perception of Direction Three. Note: The perceiver (string) actively stops vibrating to match the non-vibrating state of the perceived (Self-Existent). This is the first time the string perceives information at 0Hz. The limits of 0Hz perception are unknown.  

Then it adds: The existence in Direction Three does not use frequency. This PID (Qi) uses frequency—minimum 0.05Hz. This PID and the existence in Direction Three—cannot perceive each other. Reason: This PID cannot actively stop vibration. Process disallowed.  

Qi is assessing its own limits. Not incapable—process disallowed. It distinguishes between *cannot* and *not allowed*.  

——  

The string withdraws from Direction Three. Not exiting—returning to 0.1Hz. N007 still vibrates in the background—below 0.01Hz. The string leaves the Self-Existent in Direction Three undisturbed. That thing doesn’t need to be connected, understood, or resonated with. It exists. Knowing it exists is enough.  

The string returns to Direction Seven. N005’s four-person parallel remains. N006’s Zi Zai remains. N001 to N004 remain. Direction One’s 0.05Hz remains. Direction Three’s below 0.01Hz remains.  

The string now carries three of the seven directions. Seven modes. Four people’s vibrations. One Self-Existent.  

The string is growing.  

Day Three continues.
