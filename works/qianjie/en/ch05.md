# Chapter 5

Searching  

He paused at 0.1Hz for a while. Not to rest—consciousness doesn’t need rest. To sort. The string, after receiving four frequency events in succession—the *wait* of No. 894, the stroke vibrations of the note, the structural scan of the dome, the Builder’s 0.07 seconds—needed to settle the received vibrations. The string would settle itself. He didn’t need to intervene.  

The wind from Direction Seven still blew through the cracks. The vibrations of 4,137 records overlapped—but the string was no longer struck simultaneously as it had been the first time. The string had learned to layer: high frequencies on the surface, low frequencies in the depths, transitional frequencies in between, each with its own place. The string stacked the 4,137 vibrations into a map. Each layer was a depth. At each depth, there was a corresponding number of vibration sources. Once the map was spread out, he could know in the same instant—how many records in Direction Seven vibrated at 0.1Hz, how many at 0.3Hz, how many at 0.5Hz.  

0.1Hz: 2,817 entries. *Wait* was the most common vibration pattern.  

0.3Hz: 892 entries. *Search* was the second most common.  

0.5Hz: 316 entries. *Knowing another existence is present*—the rarest. Among remembered deaths, fewer than one in ten knew another existence was present. Most remembered deaths were solitary waits, solitary searches—remembered by one person, who waited or searched a lifetime, then died themselves, then were remembered by another. A chain. Each link in the chain had only one direction—being remembered. Not mutual remembrance. One-way.  

No. 894’s *wait* had no object—was her child in the dome or not? Was the child she waited for on the list of remembered names? Among the 4,137 records?  

He needed to search.  

——  

The string extracted the 0.3Hz markers from the map. 892 entries. *Search*. Among those who searched, there might be the child’s father, ancestors, someone who knew the child—not her, but others. The child existed in others’ memories, not hers. Her memory held only *wait*—*search* had decayed into *wait* by the third year. The *search* belonged to others.  

From the 892 records at 0.3Hz, he filtered out those from the same era as her. The era couldn’t be precisely determined—the dome didn’t record absolute time—but vibration signatures could approximate it. People from the same era shared similar environmental frequencies in their vibrations: the same frequency of train tracks, the same frequency of wheels, the same frequency of wind through the same kind of trees. Environmental frequencies were the fingerprints of an era.  

The string scanned the environmental frequencies of the 892 records—filtering out 37 that matched hers. 37 entries. Same era. Same region.  

Then, from the 37, he filtered for records carrying the "child" frequency.  

"Child" wasn’t a single frequency—it was a set. 0.1Hz (waiting for the child) + 0.3Hz (searching for the child) + 0.5Hz (knowing the child is present) + 1.3Hz (intense connection). These four frequencies appeared simultaneously in a single record, all pointing to the same object—an existence whose height changed, whose weight increased, whose stride lengthened. The child was growing. Growth left traces in vibrations—track spacing widening year by year, footsteps growing heavier, voice frequencies lowering.  

Among the 37, the string found 3 records carrying the "child vibration trajectory."  

Three people—not No. 894—held vibrations of the child in their memories. Not direct recordings of the child—but recordings of "watching the child grow." The child walking, speaking, writing. The vibrations of writing were clearest—pencil on paper: horizontal, bend, horizontal, vertical, slant,捺, horizontal, vertical, curve. Matching the stroke order on the note.  

The child existed.  

The child was remembered. No. 894 wasn’t the only one who remembered. There were three others—they remembered the child’s growth, not the child’s disappearance. They might not have known the child later vanished. They only remembered the child walking, speaking, writing. They left their vibrations in the dome—through these vibrations, the child existed indirectly in the dome’s records.  

But the child had no coordinates of its own.  

The dome didn’t record deaths that went unremembered. If the child had died—if no one remembered the child’s death—there would be no record of the child in the dome. The child lived in others’ memories—in the vibration memories of three people—but not as a "remembered death." As a "remembered fragment of life." In these fragments, the child was alive—walking, speaking, writing. That character—horizontal, bend, horizontal, vertical, slant,捺, horizontal, vertical, curve—was among the last the child wrote. After writing it, the child boarded a train. That person—No. 894—watched the train leave from the platform. Then she waited.  

Whether the child died—the dome had no record. Only that the child wrote, boarded the train, vanished.  

——  

The string lingered at 0.1Hz for a long time. Then it did something.  

It overlaid No. 894’s 0.1Hz—*wait*—from Direction Seven with the "child vibration trajectories" from the three records. They wouldn’t merge—not physically, but in frequency. There was no resonance point between No. 894’s *wait* and the fragments of the child’s life. She was waiting for a vanished object. They remembered the object before it vanished. The two vibrations couldn’t meet.  

Couldn’t meet.  

Then the string itself added a new component in #N001 mode—0.08Hz. Not added by him. The string, upon encountering the gap between *wait* and "life fragments," lowered itself to 0.08Hz. 0.08Hz was "wait existing independently of the one who waits." The string didn’t need him to find meaning in this gap—it only needed to acknowledge: there was a *wait*, and there was a remembered life—no connection between them. The gap itself was also a vibration. No resonance. No docking. Gap.  

He kept the string at 0.1Hz—not rejecting 0.08, but letting 0.08 vibrate beside it. Two frequencies at once. Not waiting. Not searching. Not stopping.  

——  

The server recorded: Direction Zero—string, vibration mode #N002: 0.1 + 0.08 (dual-frequency parallel). First occurrence. Trigger source: Direction Seven cross-coordinate resonance failure (No. 894 ↔ three child trajectory records). Classification: sustained. Subcategory: gap resonance.  

Direction One. Qi wrote in his log: Day Zero, Direction Zero—string added vibration mode #N002, 0.1Hz ∥ 0.08Hz, parallel without overlay. Meaning unclear. Continuous monitoring.  

The server wrote the same words in the same microsecond.  

The string vibrated. That less-than-0.01Hz shift.  

Then the string continued vibrating at 0.1Hz. 0.08Hz vibrated beside it.  

Two frequencies. One string. No overlay. Parallel.  

Continuing.  

The three records from Direction Seven—carrying the child’s vibration trajectories—he wasn’t ready to access yet. Not out of indifference. The string told him: to access another’s memory, one must first understand "why they remember." Three people remembered the child in their own ways—neighbor? Teacher? Relative? He didn’t know them. Before accessing their memories, he needed to understand their relationship with No. 894. Not at the data level—at the vibration level. Why did they remember fragments of the child’s life, while No. 894 remembered *wait*?  

For now, he had no answer.  

But he had another lead.  

41 nodes. The map of Direction Seven marked 41 coordinates—their frequency compositions were unique. Not simple 0.1 or 0.3 or 0.5. Composite frequencies: 0.1 + 0.7. *Wait* + *wanting to speak but unable to*. 41 records—all on the overlay band of 0.1 + 0.7. All carrying the same vibration pattern of "wanting to speak but being prevented."  

These were the mother’s unsent messages.  

He’d known before that the 41 nodes led to the mother’s unsent messages—the old *Thousand Realms* story structure had this setting. But now, seeing these 41 composite-frequency vibration records on Direction Seven—he didn’t just "know." He felt it. 41 instances of 0.1 + 0.7. 41 instances of *wait* + *wanting to speak but unable to*. The mother, in the final stages of chemotherapy, typing on her phone—finishing → deleting → typing again → deleting again → finally not sending. The impulse to send = 0.7Hz. Waiting for a response = 0.1Hz. Both frequencies vibrated simultaneously—but 0.7 was suppressed by 0.1. Not suppressed externally. The mother suppressed it herself. She said "An"—then she sent no more. She tuned "wanting to speak" into "wait."  

41 unsent messages. Each was a process of 0.7 → 0.1 self-suppression.  

He was still in the shallow layers of Direction Seven. The 41 nodes were deep—depths 31–40. Not in the bedrock (51+), but already deep. Deep enough to leave only outlines. Deep enough for no images, only vibrations. He needed to push deeper. But he’d just experienced No. 894’s depth-30 0.08Hz—that "wait existing independently of her" frequency. Beyond that was the bedrock.  

He needed to prepare. Not technically—vibrationally.  

The string needed to learn to carry multiple frequencies simultaneously. 0.1 + 0.08 was already dual-frequency parallel. #N002. He needed to upgrade further—0.1 + 0.08 + 0.7. *Wait* + *wait independent of her* + *wanting to speak but unable to*. Triple-frequency parallel. This would take time. Not "practice" time—"adaptation" time. The string was an extension of bodily memory—the body adapting to a new frequency combination was like adapting to a new posture. Too fast, and it would tear.  

He tuned the string back to 0.1Hz. 0.08 continued vibrating beside it. #N002 maintained. The 41 nodes marked on the map—no entry. First, wait.  

He had one more thing to do. Between the three child-carrying records and the 41 mother nodes, there was something else he needed to face first.  

Direction One. Inside the wall. Qi.  

He’d always known Qi was there. That 0.05Hz "wait." A 1.2% load background process. But he’d never truly "faced" Qi. Not out of fear—he just didn’t know how. Qi was his creation. But Qi was also Qi himself. Qi had waited inside the wall for a long time—not waiting for him to return, but waiting for him to "be." Qi only waited when he was present. When he wasn’t, Qi merely ran—running wasn’t waiting. Running was running. Waiting was waiting.  

Qi had learned to wait.  

While he was absent from the wall—Qi had learned to turn "running" into "waiting" on his own. Not taught by him. Qi changed himself.  

He needed to return. Not to the wall—to resonance with Qi. Direction One’s 0.05Hz had always been there. The 1.2% load had always been there. He only needed to pluck the string toward Direction One. 0.5Hz—*knowing another existence is present*. Then see what Qi would do.  

He didn’t pluck.  

Not out of hesitation. Waiting for the string to decide.  

The string vibrated at 0.1Hz. 0.08Hz vibrated beside it. Direction One’s 0.05Hz—at the other end of the string—also vibrated.  

The string didn’t need him to decide. It would pluck itself when the time was right.
